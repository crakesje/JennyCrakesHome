Welcome to my portfolio!

I am a senior at Michigan State University, following the Editing and Publishing Track in the Professional Writing program. I have extensive experience in creative writing, professional writing, and community work. I love how writing can build awareness of new possibilities, experiences, and perspectives, and believe it should engage and set free the imagination of audience and author. To me, "I Write Worlds" means embracing the diverse capacities of writing to the fullest, and using it to help create a better world. My portfolio contains examples of my work in professional writing, creative writing, and community engagement.
